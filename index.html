The Momento Experience

More than a card.
A moment that lasts.

Traditional cards end up in a drawer. Momento cards become a keepsake — with a real, personal video message locked inside every QR code, forever.

1

Each card has a unique QR code

Printed inside every Momento card is a one-of-a-kind QR code tied to a private, secure video URL — only accessible to the people who have the card.

2

Upload from any device — no app needed

The buyer scans the QR code and records or uploads a video message directly in their browser. Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop.

3

Recipient watches, keeps, and revisits

When the recipient scans the same code, they see your video. It stays there — accessible anytime, for as long as they keep the card.
